How to Play Super Snake

  1. Use the WASD or the Arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to control the direction of your snake. Press spacebar to boost.
  2. Swipe on touch devices to change direction. Double tap for boost.
  3. Eat the food items that appear on the screen to grow longer.
  4. Avoid running into the walls or your own tail, or the game will end.
  5. Your score increases with each food item you eat.
  6. Avoid predators and obstacles to survive longer.
  7. Avoid tar and water pits to maintain your speed.
  8. Don't eat the place; tar will hurt you and reduce your size.
  9. Try to achieve the highest score possible!

Tips

  • Start slow to get a feel for the controls.
  • Focus on eating predators to grow your snake quickly.
  • Maintain a small size to navigate tight spaces and guard your tail.
  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid trapping yourself.
  • Stay alert as the snake grows longer over time.

Good luck and have fun playing Super Snake!
